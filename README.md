MAHSA Resource Models
================================

- `Actor.json` — Models activities relating to heritage locations and areas e.g. survey activities.
- `Grids.json` — Models grids for study area division.
- `Heritage Area.json` — Models group of heritage sites/locations spread over an area. It could model landscapes, cities, defined areas, etc.
- `Heritage Location.json` — Models archaeological sites, heritage buildings and any features/structure within a site.
- `Historic maps.json` — Models historical map metadata mainly from Survey of India.
- `Information.json` — Models information carriers such as images, reports, and publications.
- `Person or Organisation.json` — Models actor resources such as individual or groups of people.
- `Remotely Detected Features.json` — Models remotely detected features/sites identified identified through remote sensing techniques using historical maps, satellite imagery, or other sources which can be potential heritage locations but have not been verified.
